Tutorial: How to Download Your Timetable CSV

Follow these steps to download your timetable as a CSV file:

  1. Visit the URL https://registration.ueab.ac.ke/.

  2. On the login page, enter your username (which is your school number) and password, then click "Log In".

  3. Click the hamburger menu on the top right corner of the page.

  4. From the menu that appears, click on the "Registration" option.

  5. On the Registration page, you will see several options. Look for the fourth option from the bottom, which should be labeled "Selected Timetable".

  6. Click on "Selected Timetable". This will open your current timetable in a new window.

  7. To download the timetable as a CSV file, click the "Export" button at the top of the timetable. This will download the timetable to your computer as a CSV file.

That's it! You should now have your timetable saved as a CSV file on your computer, which you can open and view using a spreadsheet program like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.
